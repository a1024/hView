1) FFTW:	for DFT
https://fftw.org/install/windows.html

2) LIBHEIF:	for opening HEIF images
https://github.com/strukturag/libde265
https://github.com/strukturag/libheif

sail-extra-avif.dll

sail-extra-jbig.dll

sail-extra-tiff.dll

sail-extra-webpdemux.dll

sail-manip.dll
sail.dll
